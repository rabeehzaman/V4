Inventory Secondary Unit of Measure Odoo Apps

Product Secondary UOM Odoo App handles your inventory management processes and enhances flexibility when dealing with various units of measurement. With this app, users can manage and convert secondary units of measure for products, facilitating a seamless transition between primary and secondary units.

Key features of this app include the ability to input secondary quantities and select secondary units of measure in sales orders, stock picking orders, and purchase orders. This functionality simplifies inventory management and order processing, making it easier to cater to the diverse needs of your business and customers.

Features

Enable Secondary UOM on Product

User can add secondary quantity and secondary unit of measure in sale order, stock picking and purchase order.
